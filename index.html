About Us

King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard, also known as "Gizzard" "King Gizz" and "The Gizz", are an Australian rock band that originated back in 2010 over in Melbourne, Victoria.
They are very well known for bring back the classic psychedelic era of London back in the 60's into today's modern age, shipped over to the other side of the world. They explore multiple genres, stage energetic live shows and building a prolific discography, having released seventeen studio albums, seven live albums, two compilations and two EPs. They sure know how to deliver to their fans with musical entertainment, and have achieved eighteen awards in doing so.

Awards

  • Four AIR Awards
  • Two ARIA Music Awards
  • Two J Awards
  • Eight Music Victoria Awards
  • Two National Live Music Awards
